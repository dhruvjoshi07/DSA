**counting sort
    it checks the frequency of element ki woh kitni bar aya
example=we have like 
    1 4 1 3 2 4 3 7  //yha pr minimum hai 1 and max=7
    ab ek array bnaya 0 to 7 and usmei bari bari value rakhi like
    0th index->1
    4th index-> 1 //kyuki 4 hai toh usko hamne count kra ki 4 hamare pass ek hai
now,1 aya upar toh 1 hamne ek bar daal diya 0th index mei toh isko milkr 2 one ho gyaa so
    0th index->2
    phir eepat for 3,2,4,3,7
    and we get from
    1 4 1 3 2 4 3 7
    to
    0 2 1 1 1 0 0 1 //count kon kitni bar aya //beech mei 0 mtlb us index mei kuch ni hai
now new array bnkr usmei dalte rho
    1 1 2 3 3 4 4 7 //sorted
